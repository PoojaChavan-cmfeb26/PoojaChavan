1) Commission per order (12%)
SELECT order_number,
       salesperson_id,
       order_amount * 0.12 AS commission
FROM Orders;


- Multiplies each order’s amount by 12% to calculate commission.

2) Highest rating in each city with formatted output
SELECT 'For the city ' || city || 
       ', the highest rating is : ' || MAX(rating) AS result
FROM Customers
GROUP BY city;


 -Uses string concatenation (||) to format the output into a sentence.

3) Customers in descending order of rating
SELECT rating,
       customer_name,
       customer_id
FROM Customers
ORDER BY rating DESC;
